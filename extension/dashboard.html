📖 User Guide

A guide for mums and dads — no tech knowledge needed!

🤔 Which approach is right for you?

🅰 Smart Download — scans and filters as it goes, only saves your child's photos.
Takes longer to start downloading (need to approve ~100 photos first), but photos are already sorted when they arrive.
🅱 Download Everything First — saves every photo immediately, sort by face later (optional).
Fast! Everything downloads in one run. Great for getting a complete backup quickly.

🅰 Smart Download — Step by Step

1

One-time Chrome setup

Go to Chrome Settings → Downloads and turn OFF "Ask where to save each file". This stops the save-file popup appearing for every photo.

2

Log in to Storypark

Open app.storypark.com in another tab and sign in as normal.

3

Select your child and run Full History Scan

Choose your child from the dropdown, then click 🔁 Full History Scan. The extension starts working through all stories. You'll see "☕ Coffee Break" pauses — totally normal!

4

Teach the AI your child's face

Photos appear in the 👀 Pending Review tab as the scan runs. For each one:
• ✅ "This is my child" — saves the face + trains the AI
• ✗ Reject — not your child (also teaches the AI)
The more you approve, the smarter it gets! You don't have to review everything right away.

5

Photos download automatically after ~100 approvals

Once the AI is confident, photos start downloading on their own. Find them in your Downloads → Storypark Smart Saver → [Child's Name] folder.

💡 Optional head start: Before scanning, go to Settings → 🧠 Face Training and upload 10+ clear photos of your child from your phone or computer. The AI learns faster with a head start — but it's not required!

🅱 Download Everything — Step by Step

1

Turn on "Download all photos"

Go to ⚙ Settings → 📂 Download Settings and turn on "Download all photos (skip face recognition)".

2

Run a Full History Scan

Every photo downloads immediately — no reviewing needed. Each photo has full metadata embedded: original date, story text, classroom name, daycare routine (feeds, naps, nappies), GPS location of the daycare, and your child's age.

3

Sort by face later (optional — works completely offline!)

1. Settings → 🧠 Face Training → upload photos of your child from your computer
2. Settings → 📁 Link Download Folder → select your Downloads folder
3. Settings → 🧹 Clean Up Folder → moves non-matching photos to a _rejected folder
The AI model is bundled inside the extension (~7.5MB). No internet needed — runs 100% on your computer!

🔍 Offline Facial Scan — Re-scan Without Internet

In the Scan tab, alongside Download Latest and Full History Scan, there's a third button: 🔍 Offline Facial Scan. This reads your already-downloaded photos directly from your hard drive — no internet needed.

✅ High confidence → face model learns automatically

👀 Uncertain matches → go to Review tab for your decision

❌ Low confidence → model learns what NOT to match

⤴ Also rescans Rejected Matches folder — photos the AI rejected early may now score better! They appear in Review tab marked "approve to rescue"

⚡ Saves fingerprint cache → next online scan skips re-downloading photos already analysed offline (up to 20x faster)

💡 Great for the "Download Everything, Then Sort" approach: After downloading everything, upload 10 training photos from your phone → run Offline Facial Scan → model improves → photos in Rejected Matches folder get re-evaluated automatically.

⏸ If the Scan Gets Interrupted

Storypark occasionally slows things down for large accounts. Don't worry:

• Progress is saved automatically every 5 stories

• A purple ▶ Resume from story X (Y remaining) button will appear

• Click it and the scan picks up exactly where it stopped — even after closing Chrome

📥 Getting Your Photos (Smart Download mode)

In Smart mode, approved photos are held in a queue. When you're ready, click the 📥 Download N Approved button in the Review tab to save them all at once — including story pages with the educator's text.

🧠 The 4 AI Phases (Smart mode)

The AI locks downloads at first to protect against downloading the wrong child's photos. Each time you approve a photo, it gets smarter:

Phase 1 (0–10 approvals) — Learning mode. All photos go to review. No downloads yet.
Phase 2 (10–50 approvals) — Getting better. Most still go to review. No downloads yet.
Phase 3 (50–100 approvals) — Almost there. Fewer reviews needed. No downloads yet.
Phase 4 (100+ approvals) — 🎉 Fully confident! Photos download automatically from now on.

💾 Save a Backup (Highly Recommended!)

Your face model and approval history are stored inside Chrome. If Chrome is ever reinstalled, you'd lose them. Go to Settings → 💾 Full Backup → Export Full Backup and save the file somewhere safe (cloud storage or USB drive). You can restore everything in seconds.

💡 Quick Tips

• Full History Scan = first time (or re-doing everything). Download Latest = check for new photos each week.

• Training photos work best when they're clear, well-lit, and show different angles of your child's face.

• Use keyboard shortcuts in the Review tab: A = Approve, R = Reject, Z = Undo.

• Photos land in Downloads → Storypark Smart Saver → [Child's Name] → Stories. Each story has its own date-named folder.

• Open the index.html file in your browser for a beautiful gallery of all your child's stories — works offline!

Downloads every photo without face matching. Useful if you just want everything.

⚠ Face recognition is disabled — all photos from stories will download, not just your child's.

Only enable if your daycare consistently submits daily routine records (feeds, naps, nappies). Leave this off if any of these apply:
• Your daycare doesn't use the routine feature
• Your child attends or has attended two daycares and one doesn't use routines
• You recently changed daycares and the new one has different practices
• You're not sure — stories from days without routine records will be silently skipped if this is on.
When off, face recognition filters your child's photos without relying on attendance records.

When on, scans only process stories that don't have any saved photos yet — skipping everything you've already downloaded.

When to use:
• You had "Skip days without routine records" turned on and want to retrieve those missed stories
• Your child changed daycares and some stories were skipped by the old settings
• Your child attends two daycares with different practices and some days were filtered out

How to use: (1) Fix the setting that was causing skips, (2) tick this option, (3) run Scan All Stories — only the gaps get filled. Then untick for normal scanning.

📁 Link Download Folder

📁 How to link your folder:
Select the "Storypark Smart Saver" folder (not your Downloads folder).
Chrome doesn't allow selecting root library folders like Downloads directly — only subfolders. Your Storypark Smart Saver folder is the correct choice.
Files are matched relative to this folder, e.g. Hugo Hill/Stories/2024-04-17 — Story/photo.jpg

🏷️ Fix Photo Metadata
Embeds data permanently into each JPEG file:
📍 GPS — daycare location (visible in Apple Photos, Google Photos, Finder)
📅 Date — story date as photo date (so photos sort by when they happened)
🏷️ Artist+IPTC — educator text, child name, room, centre (searchable in Windows/macOS)
