👶 Children

Children are automatically fetched from your Storypark account when the extension loads. Open Storypark in a tab and click Refresh if the list is empty.

🎯 Face Recognition Thresholds

Fine-tune how the AI decides which photos to download automatically and which to flag for your review.

%

Default is 85%. Photos matching this percentage or higher are downloaded automatically. 100% means perfect matches only (might skip blurry photos). Lower numbers catch more photos but risk downloading the wrong child.

%

Default is 50%. Photos matching between this number and your Auto-Approve setting are sent to 'Pending Matches' for manual confirmation. 0% means EVERY photo gets flagged for review.

📍 Centre Locations

Add GPS coordinates for each childcare centre so downloaded photos include location data in Google Photos. Your child may attend multiple centres — add one row per centre. Paste a Google Maps URL or enter coordinates directly (e.g. -27.741, 153.186).

🧠 Face Training Data

Provide 10 or more clear face photos of each child to train the recognition model. More photos, including indirect or partially-obscured ones, help build a better AI profile. Use photos where the child's face is well-lit and unobstructed where possible.

Select up to 25 photos. A live match percentage is shown to help you pick high-quality training images. Photos where no face is detected are still accepted and will be processed when you save.
