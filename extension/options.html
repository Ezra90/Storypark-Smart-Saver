🎯 Confidence Thresholds

Photos above the Auto-Approve threshold are uploaded immediately. Photos between both thresholds go to the Review Queue for manual approval. Photos below the Minimum threshold are discarded.

85%

Photos with ≥ 85% match are uploaded automatically.

50%

Photos with 50–84% match go to the Review Queue. Photos below 50% are discarded.
